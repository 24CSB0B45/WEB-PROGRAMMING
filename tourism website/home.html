WELCOME TO HYDERABAD

            

                About Hyderabad:

                Hyderabad, the capital of Telangana, is renowned for its blend of 

                historical grandeur and modern marvels. Founded in 1591 by Muhammad Quli Qutb 

                Shah, the city is a treasure trove of architectural wonders, such 

                as the Charminar, Golconda Fort, and Chowmahalla Palace. It's also famous for 

                its world-class cuisine, particularly the iconic Hyderabadi Biryani.

                Top attractions include:
                
                *Charminar: The symbol of Hyderabad, a stunning four-towered monument.

                *Golconda Fort: A sprawling fortress with a rich history and incredible acoustics.

                *Ramoji Film City: The largest film studio complex in the world.

                *Hussain Sagar Lake: A picturesque spot with a massive Buddha statue in the center.

                *Laad Bazaar: Perfect for shopping traditional bangles and pearls.
